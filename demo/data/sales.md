## Executive Summary  

Jokes Company (hereafter **JC**) continued its rapid expansion in the novelty‑gift market throughout **2025**, delivering **US $48.9 M** in total revenue—a **23 %** year‑over‑year increase versus 2024. The growth was driven primarily by the **“Giggle‑Tech”** product line (smart joke‑dispensing devices) and **strong penetration in the North‑American and European markets**.  

For **2026**, JC targets **US $63.0 M** in revenue (≈ 29 % growth) by leveraging:  

* **New “Laugh‑VR” immersive comedy experiences** (Q2 launch).  
* **Expansion of the “Pun‑Pack” subscription service** into APAC.  
* **Increased direct‑to‑consumer (DTC) e‑commerce spend** through performance‑marketing and AI‑driven personalization.  

If the outlined initiatives are executed as planned, JC anticipates a **gross margin improvement from 51 % to 55 %** and a **EBITDA margin rise from 12 % to 15 %** by year‑end 2026.

## 2025 Highlights & Lessons Learned  

| # | Highlight | Impact | Lesson / Action |
|---|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Giggle‑Tech 2.0 launch (Q3)** – integrated AI‑joke engine. | + US $ 5.3 M incremental revenue; margin uplift of 4 pp. | Keep investing in AI‑driven product upgrades. |
| 2 | **Pun‑Pack subscription** grew to 140 k active subscribers. | Recurring revenue up 18 %. | Focus on churn reduction (currently 6 % YoY). |
| 3 | **DTC channel** accounted for 42 % of sales, up from 35 % in 2024. | Higher margin (58 % vs 45 % wholesale). | Allocate 20 % more marketing spend to paid social & affiliate programs. |
| 4 | **Supply‑chain disruptions** (Q2 semiconductor shortage) raised COGS by 1.8 %. | Margin compression in Q2. | Secure secondary component suppliers and negotiate long‑term pricing contracts. |
| 5 | **“Holiday Ha‑Ha” campaign** (Nov‑Dec) drove 15 % of Q4 revenue. | Seasonal boost; high CPA (US $ 44). | Refine targeting to improve RoAS; introduce early‑bird offers. |
| 6 | **Entry into APAC e‑commerce** via Alibaba partnership (July). | First‑time revenue from region: US $ 1.8 M. | Expand localised product catalog and logistics capabilities. |

### 4.3 Risk Assessment & Mitigation*  

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Component Shortage (semiconductors)** | Medium | Margin compression (−2 pp) | Dual‑source strategy; long‑term contracts; buffer inventory. |
| **Regulatory Changes (data‑privacy for AI jokes)** | Low | Potential product redesign | Early compliance review; privacy‑by‑design architecture. |
| **Currency Fluctuations (EUR, CNY)** | Medium | Revenue volatility (±3 %) | Use forward contracts; price‑adjustment clauses for APAC contracts. |
| **Competitive Entry (large tech firms into humor market)** | High | Market share erosion | Accelerate product roadmap; invest in brand loyalty programs. |
| **Macro‑economic slowdown** | Medium | Reduced discretionary spend | Diversify into lower‑price SKUs; enhance subscription stickiness. |

\*Risk matrix based on internal risk‑management committee (Q1 2025).
