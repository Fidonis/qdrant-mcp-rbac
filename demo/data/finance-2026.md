## Notes to the Financial Statements  

1. **Basis of Presentation** – The statements have been prepared in accordance with **U.S. GAAP** and are for informational purposes only.  
2. **Revenue Recognition** – Revenue is recognized when control of the promised service or product transfers to the customer, consistent with ASC 606.  
   - **Streaming subscriptions** (48 % of total revenue) are recognized ratably over the subscription period.  
   - **Merchandise sales** (22 % of total revenue) are recognized at the point of sale.  
   - **Live events and licensing** (30 % of total revenue) are recognized upon delivery or contract completion.  
3. **Intangible Assets** – Consist of **content libraries, trademarks, and goodwill**. FY 2026 includes acquisition of **StudioTech Entertainment** for **$2.1 bn** (finalized Q2 2026). Goodwill of **$1.4 bn** is tested for impairment annually; no impairment was recorded in 2026.  
4. **Share-Based Compensation** – Stock‑based awards are measured at fair value on the grant date using the Black‑Scholes model. The expense is amortized over the vesting period (average 3.5 years).  
5. **ComedyTech Joint Venture** – The interactive comedy-gaming platform, launched in Q4 2026, contributed **$340 m** in revenue and is on track for **$800 m+ in FY 2027**.  
6. **Income Taxes** – Effective tax rate of **30 %** reflects statutory rates, deferred tax assets, and valuation allowances. FY 2026 benefited from **$75 m in tax credits** related to R&D investments.  
7. **Dividends & Share Repurchases** – The board authorized a **dividend of $1.88 per share** (25 % increase) and a **$700 m share‑repurchase plan** for FY 2027.  
8. **Contingent Liabilities** – The company is subject to routine regulatory reviews in various jurisdictions. Management does not anticipate material financial exposure.
